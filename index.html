03 — Projects

Selected Work

01 2024–2025

Solar-Powered Green Hydrogen Generation

Dissertation-level research exploring the viability of hydrogen production via solar-powered electrolysis. Designed and modelled AWE and PEM electrolysis systems integrated with solar PV, optimising electrode geometry and system efficiency. Developed a 3D-printed gyroid electrode achieving a 376% improvement in hydrogen production rate over flat plate electrodes.

Key Contributions

  • Thermodynamic & electrochemical modelling using MATLAB & Python
  • FEA-based electrode geometry optimisation (gyroid structure)
  • Comparative energy matrix: combustion, fuel cell, and mechanical recovery
  • PEM vs AWE electrolysis performance analysis under solar input
MATLAB Python SolidWorks FEA Thermodynamics Electrochemistry
